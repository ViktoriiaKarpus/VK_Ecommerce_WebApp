description

Introducing our Relaxed-Fit Cotton Denim T-Shirt, a versatile addition to your wardrobe. Crafted with care, it boasts a classic round neckline and comfortable short sleeves, making it an ideal choice for casual wear. What sets this T-shirt apart is its unique design – an asymmetric hem with stylish side vents and a subtle faded effect, adding a touch of individuality to your look. But our commitment to excellence doesn't end with fashion; it extends to our dedication to social responsibility and environmental consciousness. We take pride in our collaboration with monitoring programs that ensure strict compliance with our social, environmental, and health and safety standards throughout the production of our products. Our rigorous program of audits and continuous improvement plans guarantees that every aspect of our manufacturing process meets these high standards. The outer shell of this T-shirt is crafted from 100% cotton, with at least 20% of it being RCS Certified Recycled Cotton. This innovative material is derived from recycled cotton textile waste, reducing the need for new raw materials and promoting sustainability. It's proudly certified to the Recycled Content Standard (RCS), which traces and verifies the recycled content from its source to the final product. We believe that caring for your clothes is a way of caring for the environment. To extend the life of your denim garments, we recommend turning them inside out and washing at lower temperatures. This not only preserves their vibrant colors and fabric structure but also contributes to reduced energy consumption – a small step toward a greener world. Elevate your style with our Relaxed-Fit Cotton Denim T-Shirt while making a positive impact on both your wardrobe and the planet.
